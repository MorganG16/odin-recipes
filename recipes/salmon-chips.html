Descitpion

Fish and chips, classic dish of the British Isles, consisting of battered and deep-fried fish, usually salmon or cod, and french fries.

Ingridients

  • Salmon
  • French Fries
  • Butter
  • Seasonings
  • Dinner rolls (Optional)

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven for 5mins
  2. Grab oven tray and place foil over
  3. Place Salmon on seperate foil and add preferred seasonings with butter
  4. Place chips around the salmon on the foil
  5. Cook in oven for 25-30mins
  6. Additionally if you have dinner rolls, place in oven and let it heat for 10-15mins
  7. When food is prepared you may serve and enjoy!!
