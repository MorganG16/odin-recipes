Descirption

In its simplest form, curry is an Indian gravy or sauce that is used in tandem with meat, tofu, or vegetables. It's served rice, most popularly Basmati rice, and contains many different kinds of spices.

Ingridients

  • Rice
  • Coconut cream
  • Chicken Breast
  • Seasonings of choice
  • Curry paste
  • Oil
  • Onion
  • Mango Chutney
  1. Place the rice in the rice cooker, fill with water and add coconut cream
  2. Chop an onion on chopping board and dice chicken breast
  3. Start cooking rice and turn on stove and put oil in pan
  4. When the oil is hot place onions inside and season with preffered seasonings
  5. Once the onions start cooking place diced chicken inside and start mixing
  6. When the chicken starts to cook put two spoons of curry paste inside and stir for another 5-10mins
  7. When chicken is almost cooked add a bit of coconut cream for additional flavour
  8. Once the rice is ready, serve the rice first then serve the curry on top
  9. Enjoy your meal!! if the curry is to spicy you can add mango chutney on the side to sweeten it up
